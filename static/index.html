About

STROMDAO developed an electricity tariff for Germany where customers receive loyalty points (=Corrently) per Kilo-Watt-Hour power consumption.

Customers receive an option to exchange Corrently for a recuction of their future electricity bills. In order to achive those reductions profit sharing contracts with renewable energy operators exist.

Corrently - Invest Token

A ERC20 compliant token that bridges the gap between profit sharing contracts with operators and customers option.

Terms and Conditions

Terms of Issuing

New tokens get issued to legal contract owner as soon as a profit sharing contract exists. By publishing those contracts STROMDAO ensures that issued tokens are backed with existing profit sharing agreements (=proof of existence of legal contracts).

  • Minium 4 years term
  • 2000 Corrently per 1€ monthly profit share
  • Only renewable energy sources get accepted

Terms of Payback

If customers select to spend their collected Corrently for a reduction of future electricity bills, STROMDAO has to transfer the equivalent amount of token to the customer (2000 Tokens for a monthly reduction of 1€).

All token holders have the freedom to share, trade, sell and buy Corrently Invest Tokens at any time.

  • Customers could reduce their bills further by buying additional tokens from the market.
  • Operators (party that shares profit) could buy back obligations they sold.

Process flow

(note: Corrently invest token has 2 decimals!)

STROMDAO confirms profit share of a provider

  1. STROMDAO does assesment and valuation (=10€ per month)
  2. Issue 2000 new tokens
  3. transfer 2000 tokens to provider

Customer change of loyalty points into reduction

  1. Customer exchanges 200 Corrently
  2. Transfer of Corrently from provider to customer
  3. STROMDAO ensures electricity bill of customer is reduced by 0,10€ each month
