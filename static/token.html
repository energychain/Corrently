About

STROMDAO developed an electricity tariff with a loyalty progamme allowing to do micro investments into renewable energy generation facilities. Primary purpose of Corrently Invest Token (CORI) is a profit distribution coming out of those micro investments. CORI shareholders get their energy bills deducted by the shared profit automatically. Shareholders without an electricity tariff could request a fiat currency payout monthly by filing an invoice to STROMDAO not later than 60 days after end of month (disagio, tax and handling applies).

Terms and Conditions

Terms of Token Issuing (mint process)

New tokens get issued to legal contract owner as soon as a profit sharing contract exists. By publishing those contracts STROMDAO ensures that issued tokens are backed with existing profit sharing agreements (=proof of existence of legal contracts).

High Level Due Dilligence
  • Minium 4 years term
  • Caped 2000 Tokens per 1€ monthly profit share
  • Only renewable energy sources are accepted
  • Risk mitigation plan enforced
  • Electricity grid connection and operation procedure documented and in operation.

If asset is accepted STROMDAO (Identified by ethereum address 0x9707f3c9ca3c554a6e6d31b71a3c03d7017063f4 ) issues new tokens to the asset owner.

Smart Contract Implementation

STROMDAO redemption and profit sharing policy

Shareholders within a valid STROMDAO tariff receive shared profit automatically (aprox. 2000 Tokens required for a monthly reduction of 1€, based on actually generation in payment cycle).

All token holders have the freedom to share, trade, sell and buy Corrently Invest Tokens (CORI) at any time.

  • Customers could reduce their bills further by buying additional tokens from the market.
  • Operators/physical asset owners (party that shares profit) could buy back obligations they sold.

Clearing is done monthly within the consens of a single block in Ethereum public blockchain (aka: Homestead). STROMDAO is entitled by its customers (electricity consumers) to collect debits from property owners and deduct customer electricity bills.

Process flow (PoC)

(note: Corrently invest token has 2 decimals!)

STROMDAO confirms profit share of a provider
  1. STROMDAO does assesment and valuation (=10€ per month)
  2. Issue 2000 new tokens
  3. transfer 2000 tokens to provider
Customer change of loyalty points into reduction
  1. Customer exchanges 200 Corrently
  2. Transfer of Corrently from provider to customer
  3. STROMDAO ensures electricity bill of customer is reduced by 0,10€ each month
